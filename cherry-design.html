Our Heritage

From the moment Gary Cherry stepped into an empty jewellery store in Umhlanga 1980, he saw something different. He saw a place where service meets quality and experience meets integrity.
A place where work becomes a craft and the personal experience means everything. A place in which the finished product is more than shiny piece of silver but a piece of exclusive art that mirrors the passion, originality and creativity of it's maker.
Now the passion and exuberance of youth has blended with experience and maturity culminating in the ultimate in jewellery design.
