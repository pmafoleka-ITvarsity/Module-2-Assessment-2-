Our Vision

Integrity, transparency, trust and respect will characterize all the actions of its trading divisions and corporate support functions. The personal experience of these values will lead to mutually beneficial and lasting relationships for all stakeholders whether customers, shareholders, staff or suppliers of products or services. Clothing Junction seeks to be recognized as the premier provider of quality clothing, there Constantly building on our position as a growing retailer. We further seek to enhance our stature in the market through the pursuit and successful establishment of alternative retail formats.


Mission Statement

The company seeks to satisfy growing consumer needs. Customer value is recognized as the primary motive of the business which will duly develop all philosophy, policy and activity flowing therefrom in direct support of the customer.
