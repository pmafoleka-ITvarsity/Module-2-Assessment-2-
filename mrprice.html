Our Short Story

Mr. Price, also known as MRP, is a publicly traded retail company based in South Africa, well known for its focus on value. The group retails apparel, as well as homeware and sportswear through owned and franchised stores across 14 countries, primarily in Africa.


We believe fashion is for everyone, which is why we offer the latest looks and outstanding value!


Whether you're after the need-it-now trend or an everyday essential, you'll be sure to find it at MRP. From clothing and shoes, to sleepwear and accessories, we've got the whole family covered.
