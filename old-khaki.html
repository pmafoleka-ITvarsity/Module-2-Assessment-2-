ABOUT US

ENOUGH ABOUT US. LET’S TALK ABOUT YOU. You are not what you wear. Nothing so simple could ever define you. Because, although we may never have met, there’s one thing about you that we’re sure of: you’re too free of spirit for that.
Well, that’s what we believe.
Since 1999, we’ve fought to stay true to who we are, offering quality and style as authentic as the people who wear our clothes. Garments that are original, effortless and, like you, ready for anything.
So get out there and be exactly who and what you want to be. Because what you wear is not who you are. It’s just who you are right now.
Tomorrow can wait. Because today we live free.
