ABOUT US

Introducing Pentravel
Pentravel was established in Durban in 1983 and we recently celebrated our 35th Birthday. We have 30 branches nationwide, scattered throughout South Africas’s largest shopping malls as well as a large online division. Pentravel are proud to be part of the Travel Corporation, a leading global travel brand including companies such as Trafalgar, Contiki, Uniworld, Insight Vacations, Thompsons Holidays amongst others.
The Oyster Box in Umhlanga, The Twelve Apostles Hotel & Spa in Cape Town and Bushman’s Kloof in the Cederberg are part of the Red Carnation hotel group, also owned by the Travel Corporation. Being part of such a large Travel corporation ensure we have access to exclusive deals and the best service, and after service at all times. We are a proud partner of TreadRight which supports projects that provide assistance across a range of areas such as empowering indigenous communities to protect country and culture, uncovering and preserving historical sites and minimising the impact of tourism on our precious waterways.
We pride ourselves in being South Africa’s most reputable family leisure travel brand with a history of excellence in service delivery.
