ABOUT US

Virgin Active is a chain of health clubs in South Africa, Botswana, Namibia, Italy, Australia, Singapore, Thailand and the United Kingdom.

We have a reputation for doing things differently. For revolutionising everything we touch. Why do we do this? We’re on a mission to make exercise irresistible, because we know that for most people it’s just plain hard work. You see, we want to make going to gym as fun as possible. That’s why we offer exciting group exercise classes, one-on-one training sessions, saunas, steam rooms and state-of-the-art equipment. We believe that working out doesn’t have to be all work and no play. From super comfy lounges to health food cafes and Internet stations, our spaces are also places where you can just be, feet up and fancy-free. After all, we don’t want you to like coming to the gym, we want you to love it.
